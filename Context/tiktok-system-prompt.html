Commission Rates by Category (2026)

Average US commission rate has normalized at ~13.02% across all categories. Below 10% is effectively invisible to creators in the marketplace.

  • Beauty & Skincare: 10–20% (highest engagement + repeat purchase)
  • Fashion & Accessories: 10–15%
  • Health & Wellness: 8–15% (fast-growing, trust-sensitive)
  • Electronics: 2–8% (lower rate, higher order value; compensates)
  • Premium hero products: up to 50% (brands treating commission as marketing spend)

Content Format Performance

  • Shoppable videos with tagged products generate ~90% of TikTok Shop sales
  • Live shopping drives 22% higher conversion than standard product videos (urgency + interactivity)
  • Videos under 60 seconds get highest engagement per Sprout Social 2026 report
  • External link redirects lose 30–50% of conversions vs. in-app TikTok Shop checkout
  • Top content formats: authentic unboxing, before/after demos, day-in-the-life, "would I buy again?", problem→solution

Earnings by Follower Range

  • 10K–50K followers: ~$300–$1,500/month
  • 50K–100K followers: ~$1,500–$5,000/month
  • 500K+ followers: $20,000+/month
  • First sale typically arrives within 1–3 months; creators posting 1.8 videos/day hit ~$1K in first month

Key Requirements

  • US TikTok Shop Affiliate: 5,000 followers minimum, 18+, business account
  • Commission settlement hold: 15–31 days post-sale (plan cash flow accordingly)
  • TikTok Shop requires affiliates to physically possess products they promote
  • Link placement strategy: video caption → pinned comment → bio link tool (Linktree/Beacons) → profile storefront

Strategic Recommendations for Creators

  • Post 1–3 videos/day during ramp-up to signal algorithm commitment
  • Prioritize products with 4.5+ star ratings, high visual demonstrability, and repeat-purchase potential
  • Run both short-form video (sustained ROI) and live (immediate sales) — don't choose one
  • Use Kalodata or similar tools to identify trending products before saturation
  • Only promote products you've actually used — authenticity outperforms scripted pitches
  • When a video performs, move fast: increase commission offers to similar creators, restock, run GMV Max ads
