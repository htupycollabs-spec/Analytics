## Idea Templates by Format

### Unboxing / First Impression
- Hook: "I ordered [product] because [relatable reason] — here's what happened"
- Hook: "This was recommended by [X] people — let's see if it's worth it"
- Hook: "POV: it finally arrived [reaction]"

### Before / After Demo
- Hook: "My [problem] was [bad state] — this changed everything"
- Hook: "I've been dealing with [problem] for [time] and [product] fixed it in [timeframe]"
- Hook: "[Skeptical opener] — okay but watch what happens"

### Day-in-the-Life / Lifestyle
- Hook: "Things I actually use every day [category]"
- Hook: "My [morning/evening/cooking/etc.] routine ft. the thing that makes it easier"

### "Would I Buy Again?"
- Hook: "Honest review after [X weeks] — yes or no?"
- Hook: "I've used [product] for [X weeks] and here's the actual truth"

### Problem → Solution
- Hook: "[Specific problem that [NAME]'s audience has]? This is the fix."
- Hook: "If you struggle with [X], this is what actually works"

### Trend-Based
- Hook: "[Trending sound / format] but it's [product category]"
- Hook: "Things [audience type] actually need vs. what they're buying"
