## Title Formats That Work
1. **ALL CAPS product name + 🚨DISCOUNT TYPE🚨** — Best for Saltair and beauty
   - Example: "SALTAIR HYALURONIC ACID BODY SERUM 2 PACK 🚨BACK IN STOCK🚨 🚨ON SALE🚨"
2. **Lowercase storytelling hook** — Best for viral/personal content
   - Example: "These were the shoes that broke the internet last winter..."
3. **Hashtag-heavy niche targeting** — Good for kids/mom content
   - Example: "These Owala cups are truly the best kids cups I've come across! #owala #owalakids"

---

## What Does NOT Work
- Community/milestone content ("road to 5K", "give me a follow") — 300–1,800 views max, zero conversions
- Posting without product tagged
- Videos without a clear hook or urgency
- Products outside beauty/hair/kids/home without a strong personal angle
